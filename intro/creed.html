Purpose

Our purpose is to train professional workers and leaders in a range of academic and vocational practical skills within the context of a Christian value system.

Mission Statement

We provide higher academic and professional education to our students from all sectors in order to equip, empower and uplift them, thus providing a service and a contribution to the local church and to the economic development of the community, to honour our Creator and for the extension of His kingdom.

Our e-learning programmes are based on uniquely balanced, broad-ranged and integrated learning objectives which are locally accessible, technically supported and financially affordable.

Aims of the Institution

  • To equip leaders, managers and professionals with a base of professional and personal skills for a lifetime of learning
  • To facilitate an affordable e-learning process which will encourage, guide, and correct learners from all social and economic backgrounds, within the context of a Christian value system
  • To develop applicable Objective Directed Learning programmes for presentation through e-learning to overcome geographic limitations to the learning enterprise
  • To recruit academically qualified and professionally trained staff of Christian character to express its academic integrity
  • To foster an atmosphere where ethical values, moral norms, and aesthetic principles are integrated in the learning process for a balanced perspective of life and a positive attitude
  • To recognize other Christian Institutions of repute and foster relationships with them to facilitate the provision and exchange of relevant academic resources

Objectives for Students

Through our courses and other interaction our students will ...

  • Have the opportunity to choose Christ as Saviour and Lord and to fulfil the great commission
  • Learn how to draw on a variety of Biblical and Theological concepts and themes to teach others about the principles and practice of servant leadership
  • Have been exposed to the art of investigating, critical and independent thinking, academic writing and public speaking
  • Be equipped to be successful as career professionals and as committed Christians to be a positive influence in society
  • Have developed a number of research-related skills applied to relevant occupations and a personal research work of value

Motto

    Bringing quality e-learning within your reach!

Key Values

The University has adopted and is committed to the following key values:

Applying these professional standards of excellence at all times:

  • Dedication to fairness, integrity and dignity
  • Rendering cost effective courses and services to students through optimal development of e-learning technology
  • Develop an effective and healthy institution characterised by trust and openness and a supportive climate
