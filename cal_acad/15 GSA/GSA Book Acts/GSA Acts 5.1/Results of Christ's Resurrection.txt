<h1>
Results of Christ's Resurrection
</h1>
<p><font size="+1">
A Saviour </font></p>
<p>
Who can never die again. <font color= "blue">"knowing that Christ, having been raised from the dead, dies no more. Death no longer has dominion over Him." Rom 6:9</font></p>
<p><font size="+1">
Repentance </font></p>
<p><font color= "blue">
"The God of our fathers raised up Jesus whom you murdered by hanging on a tree. Him God has exalted to His right hand to be Prince and Saviour, to give repentance to Israel and forgiveness of sins." Acts 5:30-31</font></p>
<p><font size="+1">
New birth</font></p>
<p><font color= "blue">
"Blessed be the God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ, who according to His abundant mercy has begotten us again to a living hope through the resurrection of Jesus Christ from the dead," 1 Peter 1:3</font></p>
<p><font size="+1">
Forgiveness of sin</font></p>
<p><font color= "blue">
"And if Christ is not risen, your faith is futile; you are still in your sins!" 1 Cor 15:17</font></p>
<p><font size="+1">
The Holy Spirit</font></p>
<p><font color= "blue"> 
"This Jesus God has raised up, of which we are all witnesses. Therefore being exalted to the right hand of God, and having received from the Father the promise of the Holy Spirit, He poured out this which you now see and hear." Acts 2:32â€“33</font></p>
<p><font size="+1">
No condemnation for the elect</font></p>
<p><font color= "blue">
"Who is he who condemns? It is Christ who died, and furthermore is also risen, who is even at the right hand of God," Rom 8:34</font></p>
<p><font size="+1">
Protection</font></p>
<p>
The Lord's personal fellowship and protection.<font color= "blue"> "Lo, I am with you always, even to the end of the age." Matt 28:20</p>
<p><font size="+1">
Judgement</font></p>
<p>
Proof of coming judgement. <font color= "blue">"because He has appointed a day on which He will judge the world in righteousness by the Man whom He has ordained. He has given assurance of this to all by raising Him from the dead." Acts 17:31</font></p>
<p><font size="+1">
Salvation</font></p>
<p>
Salvation from the future wrath of God.<font color= "blue"> "and to wait for His Son from heaven, whom He raised from the dead, even Jesus who delivers us from the wrath to come.." 1 Thes 1:10; Rom 5:10</p>
<p><font size="+1">
Our Resurrection</font></p>
<p>
Our own resurrection from the dead. <font color= "blue">" knowing that He who raised up the Lord Jesus will also raise us up with Jesus, and will present us with you." 2 Cor 4:14; Rom 6:4; 8:11; 1 Cor 6:14; 15:20</p>
