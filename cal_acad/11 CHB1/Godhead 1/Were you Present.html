Job 15:8 — Were you Present?

Were you present to hear the secret counsel of God? And do you limit [the possession of] wisdom to yourself?

secret: "Were you a listener in the secret council of God?" The Hebrew means properly the cushions of a divan on which counsellors in the East usually sit. God's servants are admitted to God's secrets ( Psalms 25:14 , Genesis 18:17 , John 15:15 ).

Do you limit wisdom to yourself? - Do you wish us to understand that God' s counsels were revealed to none but yourself? And do you wish that we give implicit credence to whatsoever you are pleased to speak? These are all strong sarcastic questions, and apparently uttered with great contempt.

the possession of wisdom: did you take away, or borrow, (namely, from the divine secret council) your wisdom? Eliphaz in this ( Job 15:8 Job 15:9 ) retorts Job's words upon himself ( Job 12:2 Job 12:3 , 13:2 ).
