Description of Phase 1


Starter Books

The Phase 1 programme consists of 7 modules, each with a set of text books. A set of five books are called starter books as they are resources of information or form part of every module.
They are the following, with their book codes:

A. Concise Prospectus CPR
B. Ministry Study Guide   MSG
C. Ministry Praxis 1 MPX1
D. Calvary Disciple 1 CVD1
E. Phase 1 Readers P1Ra/b

Text Books

The 7 modules consist of the following text books:.

1. Christian Basics 1 CHB1
2. Old Testament (A Survey) 1 OTS1
3. Values of Christ VCH
4. Christian Basics 2 CHB2
5. The Gospels and Acts GSA
6. Discipleship Growth 1 DGR1
7. Survey of the Letters SLT
