Foundational programmes for various professions:

Students study at Tuition Centres on a part-time basis. Each year one Phase is completed earning 80 credits. The first three years are foundational and all students follow the "Ministry" stream. Each Phase has an academic weight of 80 credits. Having earned an Advanced Diploma in Ministry, they can then brach out in several directions.
